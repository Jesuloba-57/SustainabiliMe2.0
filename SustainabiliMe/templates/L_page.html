OUR MISSION

We want to help prevent the disproportionate amount of waste and energy usage.

The average American produces 3x the amount of waste and 4x the tons of carbon emissions in comparison to the rest of the world. To cut down on these discrepancies and help the average American do more to preserve our planet.

WHAT WE DO

SustainabiliMe is an app that allows its users to track and cut down on their energy usage and waste. To have access to these services, click 'GET STARTED' in the navigation bar and sign up!
